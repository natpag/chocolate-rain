At Jacque's We Help You Rediscover Chocolate

  • Handmade Chocolate
  • Pastries
  • Savory Dishes
  • Drinks

Our chef, Jacque Torres, prides himself on culminating the perfect atmosphere for chocolate lovers. While most people are familiar with bakeries that carry pastries and sweet chocolate delicacies, the savory side of cacao is often unexplored. We offer a medley of both sweet and savory options, all made with the highest quality ingredients, to immerse you in a land of discovery.
